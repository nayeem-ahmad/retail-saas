# Epic 23: Purchase Quotation (RFQ) Management

### Epic Goal
Allow store owners to request price quotes from multiple suppliers (Requests for Quotation - RFQ) and compare them before committing to a purchase.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: RFQ Creation & Distribution** - Create and send RFQs to one or more suppliers via email or SMS.
2. **Story 2: Quote Comparison & Analysis** - View responses from different suppliers and compare cost, delivery time, and terms.
3. **Story 3: Convert to Purchase Order** - One-click conversion from a winning quote to a `PurchaseOrder`.
