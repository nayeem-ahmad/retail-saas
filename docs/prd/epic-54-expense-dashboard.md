# Epic 54: Expense Dashboard & Analytics

### Epic Goal
Provide store owners with deep visibility into operational spending and help identify areas for cost optimization.

### Epic Description
**Key Features:**
*   **Expense by Category:** Pie chart of rent vs. utility vs. payroll.
*   **Expense Trends:** Track monthly growth or reduction in operational costs.
*   **Expense-to-Revenue Ratio:** Monitor if expenses are growing faster than sales.

**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Expense Aggregation API** - Group-wise expense totals over time.
2. **Story 2: Expense Visualizations** - Category-wise breakdown charts and historical comparisons.
