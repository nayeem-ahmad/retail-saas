# Epic 82: Customer Wallets & Store Credit

### Epic Goal
Simplify refunds and prepayments by providing customers with a digital wallet for store credits and top-ups.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Wallet Transaction API** - Support for manual top-ups, usage as a payment method, and credit-based returns.
2. **Story 2: Store Credit for Returns** - Option to issue store credit to the wallet instead of a cash refund during a Sales Return (Epic 11).
3. **Story 3: POS Wallet Payment** - Integration of "Customer Wallet" as a standard payment method in the POS checkout.
