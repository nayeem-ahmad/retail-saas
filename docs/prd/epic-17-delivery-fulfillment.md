# Epic 17: Delivery & Fulfillment Management

### Epic Goal
Provide a robust system for managing the last-mile delivery of customer orders, whether received via e-commerce, phone, or in-store sales orders.

### Epic Description
This epic bridges the gap between a completed order and the physical delivery of goods to the customer. It manages delivery personnel, assignment logic, delivery charges, and real-time status updates.

**Key Features:**
*   **Delivery Personnel Management:** Maintain a list of internal or external delivery staff.
*   **Order Assignment:** Assign pending orders to specific delivery personnel.
*   **Fulfillment Workflow:** Track orders through `Pending Fulfillment`, `Processing`, `Out for Delivery`, `Delivered`, and `Returned`.
*   **Delivery Charges:** Configure flat or distance-based delivery fees.
*   **Delivery Reports:** Track delivery performance, turnaround times, and personnel efficiency.

**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Delivery Personnel CRUD** - Manage the list of staff responsible for deliveries.
2. **Story 2: Delivery Assignment & Status** - Interface for managers to assign orders and for staff to update delivery statuses via a mobile-friendly view.
3. **Story 3: Delivery Charge Configuration** - Define rules for delivery fees in Sales Settings.
4. **Story 4: Delivery Analytics** - Reports on delivery success rates, average delivery time, and personnel performance.
