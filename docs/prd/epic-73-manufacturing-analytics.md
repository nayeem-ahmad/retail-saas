# Epic 73: Basic Manufacturing Analytics

**Goal:** Provide simple insights into production costs, yields, and raw material efficiency.

---

**Story 73.1: Production Cost & Yield Report**
*   **As a** Shop Owner, **I want to** see the total cost of production and the final yield for my manufactured goods, **so that** I can ensure my pricing is profitable.
*   **Acceptance Criteria:**
    1.  A report showing "Planned vs. Actual" material consumption for completed Work Orders.
    2.  Calculation of "Unit Production Cost" (Total Material Cost / Total Quantity Produced).
    3.  Visual trend of production volume over time.
