# Epic 60: Staff Attendance Tracking

### Epic Goal
Provide a simple mechanism for store staff to log their daily attendance and for owners to monitor work hours.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: POS Clock-In/Clock-Out** - Integrate attendance logging into the daily POS login flow.
2. **Story 2: Attendance Dashboard** - Manager view of current staff presence and monthly summaries.
3. **Story 3: Leave Request Workflow** - Digital submission and approval of staff leave.
