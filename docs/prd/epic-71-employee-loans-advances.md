# Epic 71: Employee Loans & Advances

### Epic Goal
Manage the common retail practice of providing staff with salary advances or small loans and tracking their repayment.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Loan/Advance Recording** - Record a disbursement to an employee.
2. **Story 2: Auto-Deduction Logic** - Flag a loan for automated deduction during the next N payroll cycles.
3. **Story 3: Loan Ledger** - Per-employee view of total borrowed, total paid, and outstanding balance.
