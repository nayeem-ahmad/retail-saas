# Epic 41: Lost/Stolen Product Management (Shrinkage)

### Epic Goal
Provide a specific mechanism for recording stock losses due to theft, damage, or unknown shrinkage, separately from normal sales or returns.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Loss Recording UI** - Simple form to select a product and warehouse, and record the lost quantity.
2. **Story 2: Loss Categorization** - Categorize losses by reason (e.g., `Theft`, `Damage`, `Expiration`).
3. **Story 3: Loss Reports** - High-level summary of shrinkage value and frequency by category.
