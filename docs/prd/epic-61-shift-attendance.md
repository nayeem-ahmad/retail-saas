# Epic 61: Shift & Attendance Tracking

### Epic Goal
Monitor staff presence and work hours through integrated clock-in mechanisms and shift scheduling.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Shift Definition** - Create reusable shifts (e.g., "Morning: 8 AM - 4 PM").
2. **Story 2: POS Clock-In/Out** - Lightweight attendance toggle directly on the POS interface.
3. **Story 3: Attendance Logs & Overtime** - View daily logs and automatically flag late arrivals or overtime hours.
