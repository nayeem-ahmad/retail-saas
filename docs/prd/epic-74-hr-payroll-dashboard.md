# Epic 74: HR & Payroll Dashboard

### Epic Goal
Provide managers with a view of staff attendance, leave status, and payroll trends.

### Epic Description
**Key Features:**
*   **Daily Attendance Snapshot:** Who is currently clocked in vs. late or absent.
*   **Pending Leave Approvals:** List of staff requests requiring action.
*   **Payroll Disbursement Trends:** Monthly salary payout history.

**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Attendance/Payroll Summary API** - Real-time staff presence and payroll data aggregation.
2. **Story 2: HR Dashboard UI** - Staff presence widget and leave management overview.
