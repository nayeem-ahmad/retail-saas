# Epic 34: Accounting & Financial Dashboard

### Epic Goal
Provide a clear view of the store's financial standing, including cash flow, profit margins, and tax liabilities.

### Epic Description
**Key Features:**
*   **Cash Flow Chart:** Real-time inflow vs. outflow.
*   **Net Profit vs. Gross Margin:** Track the actual health of the business.
*   **Accounts Receivable/Payable:** Visualize what is owed to the business vs. what the business owes.
*   **Tax/VAT Liability:** Real-time tracking of tax collected.

**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Financial KPI API** - Aggregate debits/credits to provide real-time ledger summaries.
2. **Story 2: Accounting Dashboard UI** - Interactive financial tiles and comparison charts.
