# Epic 80: Customer Management & Loyalty

### Epic Goal
Build deep customer relationships by tracking purchase history and rewarding repeat business through loyalty programs.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Centralized Customer Profiles** - Manage contact details and preferences.
2. **Story 2: Transaction History & Analytics** - View per-customer spending habits.
3. **Story 3: Simple Loyalty Points System** - Earn and redeem points based on purchase value.
