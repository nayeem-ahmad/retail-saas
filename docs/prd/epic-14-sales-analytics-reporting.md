# Epic 14: Sales Dashboard # Epic 14: Sales Analytics & Reporting Reporting

### Epic Goal
Provide comprehensive data visibility across all sales activities, enabling owners to track performance by product, customer, and time period.

### Epic Description
This epic focuses on aggregation and data visualization for the following modules:

**Summary Reports:**
*   **Sales Summary:** Daily/Monthly revenue and volume.
*   **Sales Return Summary:** Value and frequency of returns.
*   **Sales Order Summary:** Pipeline of pending vs. fulfilled orders.
*   **Sales Quotation Summary:** Conversion rates from quotes to sales.

**Advanced Analytics:**
*   **By Customer:** Top spenders and customer purchase frequency.
*   **By Product/Group/Subgroup:** Identify top-selling and high-margin categories.
*   **Trend Analysis:** Compare period-over-period performance (e.g., this week vs. last week).

**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Unified Reporting API** - High-performance aggregation endpoints for all sales entities.
2. **Story 2: Sales Summary Dashboard** - Interactive charts for daily KPIs.
3. **Story 3: Dimension-based Filtering** - Dynamic filtering by Warehouse, Product Group, and Customer.
