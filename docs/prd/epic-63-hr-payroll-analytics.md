# Epic 63: HR & Payroll Analytics

**Goal:** Provide business owners with a clear, visual overview of their team's performance, attendance trends, and payroll costs.

---

**Story 1: Staff Presence & Attendance Dashboard**
*   **As a** Store Manager, **I want to** see a real-time snapshot of my staff, **so that** I know exactly who is working and who is absent today.
*   **Acceptance Criteria:**
    1.  A dashboard widget showing current staff status: "Working", "On Break", "Absent", "On Leave".
    2.  Monthly attendance trends (Punctuality %, Absence rate).

---

**Story 2: Payroll & Cost Analytics**
*   **As a** Shop Owner, **I want to** see a visual breakdown of my payroll costs over time, **so that** I can manage my business's largest operational expense.
*   **Acceptance Criteria:**
    1.  Monthly salary payout trends (Total Payout vs. Previous Month).
    2.  Breakdown of costs by department or designation.
    3.  Summary of outstanding loans and advances across the whole team.
