# Epic 13: Sales Quotation Management

### Epic Goal
Allow store owners to provide formal price quotes to customers, which can be easily converted into Sales Orders or POS Transactions.

### Epic Description
**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Quotation Creation & PDF** - Build professional quotes with expiration dates and generate PDF documents.
2. **Story 2: Quotation Versioning** - Track revisions made to a quote during negotiation.
3. **Story 3: Convert to Order/Sale** - One-click conversion from a `Quotation` to a `SalesOrder` or `Sale`.
