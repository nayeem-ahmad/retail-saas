# Epic 62: Payroll & Salary Management

**Goal:** Simplify the monthly payroll process for small businesses, including salary setup, advances, and one-click generation of payslips.

---

**Story 1: Salary Profile & Loan Management**
*   **As a** Shop Owner, **I want to** define base salaries and manage staff advances, **so that** I have a clear financial structure for my team.
*   **Acceptance Criteria:**
    1.  A "Salary Profile" for each employee defining: Basic Pay, Fixed Allowances (e.g., Medical, Transport), and recurring Deductions.
    2.  A simple tool to record "Salary Advances" or "Loans" with an option for automated repayment during payroll.
    3.  A loan ledger per employee showing total borrowed, paid, and outstanding.

---

**Story 2: Monthly Payroll Generation**
*   **As a** Store Manager, **I want to** generate the monthly payroll with a single click, **so that** I don't have to manually calculate net pay for every employee.
*   **Acceptance Criteria:**
    1.  The system automatically calculates Net Salary based on: Base Pay + Allowances - Deductions - Loan Repayments - Attendance Penalties (if any).
    2.  A "Payroll Summary" review screen before final processing.
    3.  Integration with the Accounting module to automatically generate a salary disbursement voucher (Epic 30).

---

**Story 3: Digital Payslips & Notifications**
*   **As an** Employee, **I want to** receive a digital record of my salary payment, **so that** I have transparency regarding my earnings and deductions.
*   **Acceptance Criteria:**
    1.  Automated generation of a simple, printable/PDF payslip for each employee.
    2.  Optional notification via SMS or Email (Epic 83) when payroll is disbursed.
    3.  Employees can view their historical payslips through their profile.
