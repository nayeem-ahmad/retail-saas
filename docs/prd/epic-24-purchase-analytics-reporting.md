# Epic 24: Purchase Dashboard # Epic 24: Purchase Analytics & Reporting Reporting

### Epic Goal
Provide a comprehensive view of procurement activities, supplier performance, and stock replenishment trends.

### Epic Description
This epic focuses on aggregation and data visualization for the following modules:

**Summary Reports:**
*   **Purchase Summary:** Total procurement value and volume.
*   **Purchase Return Summary:** Value and frequency of returns to suppliers.
*   **Purchase Order Summary:** Pipeline of pending vs. received POs.
*   **Purchase Quotation/RFQ Summary:** Quote conversion and negotiation success rates.

**Advanced Analytics:**
*   **By Supplier:** Top suppliers by volume, spend, and lead time.
*   **By Product/Group/Subgroup:** Identify procurement trends and cost fluctuations.
*   **Price History Analysis:** Track how cost prices for specific products change over time.

**Stories:**
1. **Story 1: Unified Procurement Reporting API** - Aggregation endpoints for all purchase entities.
2. **Story 2: Supplier Performance Dashboard** - Interactive view of supplier reliability and spend.
3. **Story 3: Cost-Trend Visualization** - Charts showing product price history across different suppliers.
