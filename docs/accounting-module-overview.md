# Accounting & User Management — Module Overview

*A complete double-entry accounting system built for Bangladeshi retail, with enterprise-grade team and access controls included.*

---

## Who this is for

You run a retail business and want proper books — not a spreadsheet. You want
to know your real profit, what customers owe you, what you owe suppliers, and
you want to hand a clean trial balance to your auditor at year-end. You also
want your staff to use the system without seeing everything or breaking
anything.

This module gives you a full general ledger with automatic posting, the
financial reports your accountant expects, and a role-based team system so the
right people see the right things. It is designed around how shops in
Bangladesh actually operate — BDT, NBR VAT returns, cash-and-mobile-wallet
payments, and export to the accounting software your auditor already uses.

---

## What you get — Accounting

### The books, kept automatically

- **Chart of Accounts** — a ready-made account structure (Assets, Liabilities,
  Equity, Revenue, Expenses) you can extend with your own groups and accounts.
- **Automatic posting** — when you make a sale, record a purchase, pay an
  expense, transfer funds, or take a customer payment, the correct
  double-entry journal is created for you. No manual bookkeeping for everyday
  transactions.
- **Manual vouchers** — Cash & Bank Payment/Receive, Fund Transfer, and Journal
  vouchers when you need them, with automatic voucher numbering and a
  built-in check that debits always equal credits.
- **General Ledger** — every account's full history with running balances.
- **Opening balances** — migrating from another system or from paper? Import
  your starting balances and carry on.

### Reports your accountant will ask for

| Report | What it tells you |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Profit & Loss** | Are you making money — and where it's going |
| **Comparative P&L** | This period vs. last period vs. last year |
| **Balance Sheet** | What you own and owe, on any date |
| **Trial Balance** | The pre-audit summary every accountant starts from |
| **Cash Flow Statement** | Where cash actually came from and went |
| **Cashbook & Bankbook** | Cash and bank movement with running balances |
| **Financial Ratios** | Current ratio, margins, days-to-collect/pay, at a glance |
| **Budget vs. Actual** | How you're tracking against plan |
| **Cost-Center P&L** | Profit by branch or department |

### Money in, money out

- **Customer ledger & payments** — track credit sales, receipts, and balances
  per customer, with **AR Aging** (0–30 / 31–60 / 61–90 / 90+ days) so you
  know who's overdue.
- **Supplier ledger & payments** — the same for what you owe, with **AP Aging**.
- **Expenses** — categorised expense entries with their own reports, posted to
  the ledger automatically.
- **Loans** — track money you've borrowed and money you've lent, with
  repayment schedules.
- **Fixed assets & depreciation** — register assets and let the system generate
  monthly depreciation entries (straight-line or declining-balance).
- **Salary payments** and configurable **payment methods** (cash, card, mobile
  wallet, bank) linked to the right accounts.

### Bangladesh-ready & audit-ready

- **VAT / Tax ledger** formatted for the NBR monthly VAT return.
- **Fiscal period locking** — close a month or year so no one can slip in a
  backdated entry. Unlocking is restricted to the business owner.
- **Bank reconciliation** — import your bank statement, auto-match by date and
  amount, reconcile the rest by hand.
- **Recurring journals** — set up rent, subscriptions, and other regular
  entries once; they post on schedule.
- **Cost centers / departments** — tag entries to a branch or department for
  segment-level reporting.
- **Full audit trail** on every voucher and account change — who, what, when.
- **Export to Tally and QuickBooks** — hand your auditor a file they can open
  directly.

## What you get — User Management (included)

Every plan includes a complete team and access-control system, so the
accounting module is safe to put in front of your whole staff.

### Accounts & sign-in
- Email/password sign-up with your business and first store set up
  automatically.
- Email verification and self-service **password reset**.
- **Two-factor authentication (2FA)** for extra protection on owner accounts.
- Secure sessions — changing a password or logging out signs out everywhere.

### Roles & permissions
- Four built-in roles — **Owner, Manager, Cashier, Accountant** — each with
  sensible default permissions. (An Accountant, for example, sees the ledger
  and financial reports but not your staff's payroll controls.)
- **47 fine-grained permissions** across products, inventory, sales,
  accounting, CRM, and administration.
- **Per-store, per-permission control** — grant someone access to one branch's
  sales but not its accounting, all from a simple grid.

### Team & multi-branch
- **Invite staff by email**; they accept and join with the role you choose.
- Manage who can access which store, and who can switch between stores.
- Remove members safely (the system won't let you delete the last owner).

### Oversight
- **Audit logs** of logins, password changes, team changes, and permission
  grants — filterable by person, action, and date.
- **API keys** for connecting other tools, issued and revoked by the owner.
- Multi-tenant isolation — your data is scoped to your business and stores at
  every layer.

## How it fits together

Because posting is automatic, your team just does their job — a cashier rings
up a sale, a manager records a purchase — and the books update themselves in
the background. Your accountant works from clean, reconciled ledgers and
exports to Tally or QuickBooks at period-end. You set permissions once, lock
each month when it closes, and trust the audit trail for everything else.
